Tip 1: Long term investments

The best tip anyone can give you when you start investing in the stock market is you shouldn't atempt to get rich quick. The safest option when investing is to use an ETF like the S&P 500 to recieve realistic returns like 10% a year over losing your money in a risky stock.

Tip 2: Don't over invest

Time and time again we will here stories of people losing everything they have in the stock market hopeing to get rich easy. DONT. Only invest a small percentage of your earnings we suggest at most 15% in any area of the stock market so no matter what happens you will be ok.

Tip 3: Commit to your investments

It is important to know that the stock market fluctuates as people are constantly buying or selling the stock our best advice when this happens would be to trust your research. Unless bad news comes out about the company don't panic and remember that the stock will not reach your target over night.
